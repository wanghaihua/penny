Execution Flow

How we support each order

01.Inquiry Brief

Share product type, target market, monthly demand and any packaging requirements.

02.Spec Sheet Alignment

We confirm ply, sheet size, grammage, embossing, pack count, MOQ and lead time before quoting.

03.Sample & Artwork Review

Samples, barcode placement, language panels, carton marks and pallet standards are checked early.

04.Production Tracking

Once confirmed, we follow the production schedule and keep key milestones visible.

05.QC & Loading Check

Inspection covers absorbency, grammage, count, sealing quality and loading photos before shipment.

06.Document Handover

Commercial invoice, packing list, booking updates and export documents are coordinated for import planning.
