Built around recurring tissue orders

Yame connects mill capacity, packaging detail and export execution for professional buyers.

We focus on parent rolls and finished tissue products, including toilet tissue, kitchen towels, napkins, paper towels, facial tissue and pocket tissue.

For more than 18 years, we have worked with China and Vietnam mills to support retail, away-from-home and foodservice channels. Our work is less about one-off selling and more about keeping specifications, samples, packaging and shipment details aligned for repeat orders.
